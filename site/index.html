Frequently asked

Does APT require Ollama?

Yes. APT manages models through Ollama. Install Ollama first — APT handles the rest.

Is it actually free?

The core — hardware scan, recommendations, benchmarking, chat, web UI, TUI — is MIT open source and always will be. Pro features are a paid add-on.

Will my data leave my machine?

No. Everything runs locally. No telemetry, no cloud, no accounts. Your models never leave your hardware.

What GPUs are supported?

NVIDIA (all), AMD via Vulkan (RX 6800 XT verified), Apple Silicon M-series, Intel Arc. CPU-only works too — just slower.

Why aptm instead of apt?

Debian already owns apt on Linux. Windows keeps apt; Linux/macOS use aptm (APT-manager).

Is there a GUI?

Desktop app (Windows installer) + web UI on :5050 + full terminal TUI. Pick your interface.
